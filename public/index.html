üí° Pro Tips

  • Text Contrast: Use white text (#FFFFFF) on dark backgrounds, dark text on light backgrounds
  • Font Choice: Impact is great for bold statements, Helvetica for clean modern looks, Georgia for elegant content
  • Text Length: Keep titles under 50 characters and subtitles under 100 characters for best results
  • Image Quality: Use high-resolution images (at least 1080px) for best quality output
  • Preview First: Use the preview button to check each slide before generating the full carousel
  • Batch Processing: Generate up to 10 slides at once for optimal performance

üîß Troubleshooting

Text not appearing?

Make sure you've entered both title and subtitle, or at least one of them. Check that text color contrasts with your background.

Image download failed?

Verify your image URL is publicly accessible. For Google Drive, use the /uc?id= format.

Text overflow?

Text automatically wraps, but if you see overflow, try shorter text or adjust the font size in the API.
